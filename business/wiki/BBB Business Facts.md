# BBB Business Facts

**The single source of truth for what BBB is, sells, and stands for.** When answering anything about the business, use *these* facts — never invented services, prices, or claims. Update this page as the business changes.

## Identity

- **Legal name:** BBB Mobile DOT Drug Testing LLC
- **Owner/operator:** Blaklee Blanchard — sole operator, DOT-Qualified collector and DOT-Qualified BAT (Breath Alcohol Technician)
- **What it is:** Woman-owned **mobile** drug and alcohol testing company. "We roll to you" — testing brought directly to job sites, offices, and fleets.
- **Base & service area:** Park City, UT — serving **Summit, Wasatch, and Salt Lake counties**
- **Positioning:** *Local.* Most competing mobile testing services are national companies; BBB is not.

## Ideal customer (ICP)

- **Fit is about the business, not the individual** — any profession or age.
- **Primary segments:** construction, trucking, and government/HR departments in the three-county area.
- **Trigger:** a DOT-regulated testing need (randoms, pre-employment, post-accident, return-to-work) — or a company realizing that skipping DOT-mandated testing risks serious audit penalties. See [[DOT Test Reasons — The 6 Types]].
- **Lead sources:** direct daily outreach, networking, word of mouth, TPAs (third-party administrators), direct mail and email.

## Customer pains (in their words)

1. "Clinics have wait times, then my employees waste time before getting back to work."
2. "DOT testing is regulated — it shouldn't be skipped, because it could cost serious money if audited."
3. Lost time and money to drive time and downtime when employees leave the job site to test.
