# Reading the No-Yes Ratio

Outreach produces a lot of no's right now, with some yeses mixed in. The no's don't feel good, but the yeses are proof the offer works — the ratio itself is normal for a business three weeks old, not a signal to change course.

The discipline here: don't let the volume of no's overshadow the fact that yeses are already happening. Revisit this page as the ratio shifts with more [[Outbound Outreach Rhythm|outreach volume]].
