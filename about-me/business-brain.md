## The business, in plain English

BBB Mobile DOT Drug Testing LLC is a woman-owned mobile drug and alcohol testing company serving Summit, Wasatch, and Salt Lake counties, Utah. Founded by Blaklee Blanchard, a DOT-qualified collector and DOT-qualified BAT, the company brings professional, compliant testing directly to job sites, offices, and fleets — "We roll to you." BBB is AABB-certified, HIPAA-certified, holds WOSB and EDWOSB certifications through the SBA, and is a member of NDASA.

---

## What I sell

- **DOT urine drug test:** 5-panel, SAMHSA-certified labs, MRO reviewed — $100
- **DOT breath alcohol test (BAT):** DOT-compliant protocol — $65
- **Non-DOT 5-panel drug test:** $75
- **Travel fee:** $70 per visit — waived for 5+ tests at one location, or under contract

---

## Who I sell to (ICP)

- **Primary ICP:** Any profession or age — the fit is about the business, not the individual. Construction, trucking, and government/HR departments in Summit, Wasatch, and Salt Lake counties.
- **The trigger:** DOT-regulated testing need — randoms, pre-employment, post-accident, return-to-work — or a company that's realized skipping DOT-mandated testing risks serious audit penalties.
- **Where they come from right now:** People Blaklee has met directly who see the benefit; daily outreach to companies; networking; TPAs (third-party administrators); direct mail and email to businesses.

---

## Top 3 pains of my ICP (in their words)

1. "Clinics have wait times, then my employees waste time before getting back to work."
2. "DOT testing is regulated — it shouldn't be skipped, because it could cost serious money if audited."
3. Losing time and money to drive time and downtime when employees have to leave the job site for testing.

## Key partners / channels

- **TPAs (third-party administrators):** a channel for referrals and contract work.
- **Direct outreach:** daily company outreach, networking, direct mail, and email.

## Money flow (high level)

- **Revenue model:** straight per-test pricing, plus a $70 travel fee (waived for 5+ tests at one location, or under a contract).
- **Current state:** business is 3 weeks old. ~$500 in revenue to date, 2 jobs completed, a few post-accident calls missed. All growth so far has come from personal outreach and word of mouth.
- **Where I want revenue to come from:** moving from one-off wins to a repeatable outbound pipeline of recurring testing contracts.

## Goals

- **12 months:** Recurring contracts in place, testing happening daily.
- **3 years:** Contracts with city and state government, several BBB vans running around town, professional and compliant testing available across Utah.
- **One thing that would change everything:** building a repeatable outbound pipeline that converts one-off wins into recurring contract testing.
